What is Newsminer?

NewsMiner+ is a web blog providing mashups regarding recent topics. Imagine, as different newspapers almost deal the same topics, it's rather complicated to get an overview over different articles at first and the topic bakground at second.

Technically, the application crawls a large collection of RSS feeds from all over the world, such as Le Monde, The Guardian or The New York Times. After clustering the articles, all relevant entities are detected and the NewsMiner+ searches for additional informations to describe them, using Freebase, GoogleMaps and Leaflet/Open Street Maps.
